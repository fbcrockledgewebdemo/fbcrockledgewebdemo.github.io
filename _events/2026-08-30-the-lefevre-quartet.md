## About the LeFevre Quartet

The LeFevre name has been part of Southern Gospel music for generations. Formed in 2005, the LeFevre Quartet has received industry honors, produced chart-topping songs, and built a strong following. Through powerful vocals and energetic performances, the group’s central purpose is to share the hope, grace, and salvation of Jesus Christ.

### Mike LeFevre — Baritone

A veteran vocalist from the LeFevre family, Mike has performed with the Singing Americans, Brian Free & Assurance, and Gold City. He lives near Gainesville, Georgia, with his wife, Kathy.

### Jordan LeFevre — Lead

Jordan sings alongside his father, blending traditional Southern Gospel with a fresh, contemporary approach. He and his wife, Ali, have three sons.

### Joshua Horrell — Tenor

Raised in a musical family, Joshua has performed with the Kingdom Heirs and the Kingsmen Quartet. His joyful personality and strong faith shape his ministry.

### Daniel Ashmore — Bass

Formerly with the Old Paths, Daniel is known for his rich bass voice, warm character, and commitment to sharing the Gospel.

### Jack Jordan — Pianist

Jack began playing piano and singing at age five. His ministry reflects a deep faith and a desire to encourage others through Gospel music.
