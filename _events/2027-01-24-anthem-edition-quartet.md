## About Anthem Edition

Anthem Edition is an award-winning Southern Gospel quartet known for powerful harmonies, uplifting music, and a ministry focused on sharing the message of Jesus Christ.

Originally formed in 2003 as The Old Paths, the group gained national recognition through chart-topping songs and received the Singing News Fan Award for Favorite New Quartet. In 2022, the quartet entered a new chapter under the name Anthem Edition.

The group features **Cameron Edens** singing tenor, **Tim Rackley** singing lead, **Doug Roark** singing baritone, and **Andrew Utech** singing bass. Together, they continue to encourage audiences through energetic performances, traditional quartet harmony, and songs centered on faith, hope, and the Gospel.
