Join us on Christmas Day as we celebrate the birth of Christ together. Our worship service will begin at **11:00 AM**, followed by a church lunch at **12:00 PM**. After lunch, we will continue the day in service to others by visiting a local charity, where we will give gifts and help provide a meal for those in need. We invite you and your family to take part in this meaningful day of worship, fellowship, and Christian service.
