## About the Down East Boys

For more than four decades, the Down East Boys have been a respected name in Southern Gospel music. Formed in 1985, the quartet is known for its rich harmonies, meaningful lyrics, and ministry-centered performances that share the hope of the Gospel.

The current lineup includes **Doug Pittman** singing tenor, **Ricky Carden** singing lead, **Cayden Howell** singing baritone, and **Brandon Barry** singing bass. With multiple number-one songs, numerous industry honors, and a 2025 GMA Dove Award nomination, the Down East Boys continue to encourage audiences at churches, festivals, concert halls, and Gospel music events across the country.
