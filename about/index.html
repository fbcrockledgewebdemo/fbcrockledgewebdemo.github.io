About First Baptist Church of Rockledge

A Church Family Rooted in Christ

We are a Christ-centered community committed to authentic worship, biblical teaching, and meaningful relationships. Whether you are new to the area, exploring faith, or looking for a church home, you will find a warm welcome here.

First Baptist Church of Rockledge has served Brevard County for generations, sharing the Good News of Jesus locally and globally.

What we believe

Our Beliefs

God the Father

We believe in God the Father, Creator and sovereign Lord of the universe.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ, God’s Son, lived a perfect and sinless life, died on the cross for the forgiveness of sin, rose again, and gives abundant and eternal life.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit comforts us, convicts us of sin, interprets Holy Scripture, and draws us to the Father.

Christ’s Return

We believe Jesus will physically return to receive those who believe in Him, and will live and reign for eternity.
